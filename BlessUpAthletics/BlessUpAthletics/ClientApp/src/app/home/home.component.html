About Bless Up Athletics


Bless Up Athletics LLC is a brand designed to help its athletes grow in faith with God as well as Improving basketball skills on the court.


About Me


Lance Dezee is the founder of Bless Up Athletics LLC. I am a three year letterwinner from Berlin Center Western Reserve. I went on to play two years of college basketball at Bethany College. At Western Reserve I hold the records for most 3 pointers made in a career as well as highest career free throw percentage.


SERVICES

What Bless Up Offers


Recruitment

I offer recruiting and exposure help with older athletes who are ready to start that process. I make highlight videos with their film and send it out to coaches and also contact coaches to advertise the player and also set up dialogue on their behalf.

Individual Evaluations

I focus on making the complete player. I study a player and find their strengths and weaknesses. I then design a specific off-season program for that player that details ball handling, shooting, individual offense as well as individual defense.

Strength and Conditioning

Included in your program will be strength and conditioning tailored to help you improve skills further.
